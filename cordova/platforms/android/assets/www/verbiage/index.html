How does the Rewards Program work for me?

  1. Purchases: At EverFit™ we truly want you to make the most of being part of the team through our Stack and Rack Your Weights program. 1 pound will be stacked to your barbell for every $1 you spend in our store, just like many credit cards. Keep stacking the pounds and use them as store credit towards ANY merchandise you want! FREE stuff!
  2. Affiliate program: As a User/Fanatic, you automatically get points/pounds from your own purchases, which is awesome. But in addition, you can earn additional points simply by telling other people about EverFit through your AFFILATE link! Your affiliate link can be shared on all social media accounts, email, text or any other electronic means of distribution. A new user does not have to be someone you know personally. Your affiliate link will bring them to the website and at the same time code them to your name so that you receive additional “Pounds” on YOUR barbell from THEIR purchases. That means that if some person clicks your link, who you may or may not know, and then purchased an item from the EverFit store, you get FREE Pounds that YOU can redeem as store credit. Yes, it is that simple!
