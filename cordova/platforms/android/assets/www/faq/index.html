Most Common Questions Answered

How are my commissions calculated?

Any individual Trainer can sign up an unlimited number of Clients. Upon signup every Client (now a Subscriber) is asked: “Who brought you to the LiveEverFit.com website?” They cannot join without answering this question. Once the Subscriber has selected a Trainer, they will be assigned to that Trainer for as long as they are a member of BeForeverFit.com and that Trainer will get commissions for all of the sales purchased from the website by that Subscriber.

Once the Client becomes a Subscriber, they will get unlimited access to the entire video library and the entire LiveEverFit.com website. The potential for unlimited income is based on how well each Trainer promotes themselves and the products available on our site. It is truly limitless because unlike doing a website on your own, LiveEverFit.com has power in group marketing and the ability to generate far more Clients for each Trainer because of our large online presence.

What if I already have a supplement deal or non-compete?

I have personally spoken with the owners of one of the largest supplement companies in the country about this issue and there is no violation of your contract by joining LiveEverFit.com. We are presenting all of the top supplements for our Trainers to promote in a completely unbiased manner. Due to the variety of trainers we need to have a variety of products. As long as the individual Trainer who has the supplement agreement only promotes that supplement on their LiveEverFit.com blog, there is no violation of the contract. It is not different than representing a brand that is sold in a supermarket. Just because the supermarket is selling other brands does not mean the Promoter/Trainer is violating their commitment.

What if my Gym thinks it is a violation of our contract?

LiveEverFit.com is not in competition with any gym. We are marketing to those people who want to increase their personal level of fitness. These are the same people targeted by gyms. Giving the gym’s Trainers additional marketing exposure to these potential Clients will do nothing but add to the gym’s marketing exposure for FREE. All of the Subscribers who are looking for in-person training and find a Trainer they love through LiveEverFit.com will have to join a gym; and they are going to join the gym where the Trainer is already training. That is a complete win-win-win for the Client, Trainer, Gym and LiveEverFit.com.
